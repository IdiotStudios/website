BarDar is a fast, retro-inspired pixel roguelike built in Rust — explore procedurally generated rooms, battle quirky enemies, scavenge powerful items, and unlock permanent upgrades across short, addictive runs. With crisp 320x180 pixel art, tight controls, and cross-platform support (desktop + Android), it's perfect for quick sessions or longer challenges—dive in and see how deep you can go!
