Introduction

IdiotStudios is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Visitor Data

  • Email address (if submitted via forms)
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser and device information
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent)
  • Cookies and tracking information

1.2. Account Data

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Username / Authentication UID

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Account creation and login
  • Analytics and site performance monitoring
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Service communication (emails, alerts)
  • Legal compliance

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following external services:

  • Google Analytics — for usage tracking. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — for displaying ads. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — for DNS, CDN, and security. Privacy Policy

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies for login, session management, and preferences. Optional cookies are used for analytics and advertising. You may disable cookies via your browser settings, but some functionality may be affected.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Inactive accounts may be deleted after 12 months. Some logs may be retained for legal or security reasons.

6. Your Rights

6.1. POPIA (South Africa)

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the South African Information Regulator

6.2. GDPR (EU/EEA)

  • Data portability
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • File a complaint with your data protection authority

6.3. CCPA/CalOPPA (US)

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell personal data)

Contact us at contact@idiotstudios.co.za

7. No Sale of Personal Data

IdiotStudios does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data is only used as described in this Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new effective date. Significant changes affecting your rights will be communicated via email where possible.
